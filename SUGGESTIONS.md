# Shroom Vroom — Feature Suggestions & Ideas

## High Priority

### 1. Add a Subscription/Weekly Box Plan
- Customers can subscribe for weekly/bi-weekly delivery of fresh oyster mushrooms
- Auto-recurring WhatsApp reminders or orders
- Discount for subscribers (e.g., ₹180/kg instead of ₹200)

### 2. Customer Reviews & Testimonials (Real)
- Collect reviews via WhatsApp after delivery
- Display star ratings on product cards
- Show delivery photos from happy customers

### 3. Refer & Earn Program
- Existing customers share a WhatsApp link
- New customer gets ₹50 off first order
- Referrer gets ₹50 credit on next order
- Track via unique referral codes

---

## Medium Priority

### 4. Recipe Video Shorts
- 30-60 second Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts for each recipe
- Embed in the Learn section
- Shows fresh Shroom Vroom mushrooms being cooked

### 6. Seasonal Product Drops
- Limited-edition varieties (e.g., Pink Oyster, King Oyster)
- "Coming Soon" countdown timer
- WhatsApp waitlist notification

### 7. Blog SEO Content
- "Mushroom farming in Rajasthan" — target local search
- "Best protein sources for vegetarians in India"
- "Cordyceps vs whey protein" comparison articles
- Helps drive organic traffic from Google

## Nice to Have

### 10. Loyalty Points System
- 1 point per ₹10 spent
- 100 points = ₹50 off
- Track manually via WhatsApp (no backend needed initially)

### 11. Google Business Profile Integration
- Add structured data (LocalBusiness schema)
- Show delivery radius on Google Maps
- Collect Google Reviews

### 12. Instagram Feed Widget
- Show latest @shroomvroom posts on homepage
- Social proof + fresh content without manual updates

---

## Technical Improvements

### 13. Analytics & Tracking
- Add Google Analytics 4 / Plausible for privacy-friendly analytics
- Track: page views, "Add to cart" clicks, WhatsApp checkout opens
- Helps understand which products and recipes drive orders

### 14. PWA (Progressive Web App)
- Add to homescreen on mobile
- Offline-capable product browsing
- Push notifications for delivery updates

### 15. Headless CMS Migration
- Move product data & recipes to Sanity or Contentful
- Allows non-technical team to update content via a dashboard
- No code changes needed for content updates

### 16. Payment Gateway (Future)
- Razorpay integration when order volume justifies it
- Still keep UPI/COD as options
- Auto-confirmation via webhook
